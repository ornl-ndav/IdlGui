Interaction with Live Infos activated (at least one of them)

  • ZOOM


Left click the first first corner of the selection, without releasing the left click, move to the second corner of the selection and then release the mouse.

  • RANGE SELECTION


Press SHIFT KEY and left click to select the first corner of the selection. Them without releasing the key or the mouse, moves to the second corner of the selection. The control ino, Counts vs Qx and/or Counts vs Qz display the range selected (right part of top image).

  • OUTPUT INFOS PLOTS
To be able to produce ASCII or PS files, make sure that at least one of the two infos plots are enabled (counts vs Qz or counts vs Qx or both), then right click at any position of the cursor on the plot, or with a selection activated.
